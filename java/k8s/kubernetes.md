## CART 服务搭建

- 1. **cd到安装路径**
```text
pdi-ce-9.0.0.0-423\data-integration
```
- 2. **修改配置文件** 
```text
/pwd/carte-config-**.xml
```
- 3. **指定配置文件启动**
```text
carte ./pwd/carte-config-**.xml
```
- 4. **启动完成后**
```text
 http://ip:port/
```
